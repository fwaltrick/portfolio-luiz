description_en: >
  `Catalog for the exhibition` of ceramic artist Kimi Nii at the Caixa Cultural
  Center in São Paulo. Kimi Nii was born in Hiroshima, Japan, and has lived in
  Brazil since 1957. As the daughter of Japanese immigrants, she grew up in a
  culturally diverse environment that later strongly influenced her artistic
  language.


  In her studio in São Paulo, Kimi Nii creates masterful sculptures and everyday
  objects from ceramics, combining artisanal precision with aesthetic
  sensitivity. Her works are characterized by clear lines, balanced forms, and a
  subtle color palette. The influences of her Japanese heritage are clearly felt
  and merge with Brazilian elements to create a distinctive expression.
