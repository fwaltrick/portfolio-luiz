  `For the German` feature film **Paula** (2016, directed by Christian
  Schwochow), which tells the story of painter Paula Modersohn-Becker, a
  comprehensive range of graphic materials was developed, including the **film
  poster**, **title design**, **press kit**, and **cinema advertising**.

  femininity, and emotional strength – and translates them into a design that
  bridges the historical context with a contemporary cinematic approach.
