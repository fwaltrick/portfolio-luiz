description_en: >
  `A German film drama `from 2016, directed by Chris Kraus, is at the center of
  this project. The film tells the story of Totila Blumen, a Holocaust
  researcher who must confront the horrors of his family's past. His life
  changes when he meets the French student Zazie Almaidane. The story explores
  themes such as trauma and human connection in an empathetic manner.


  For **The Bloom of Yesterday**, various promotional materials were developed,
  including posters, flyers, banners, DVDs, and other elements. These were
  carefully designed to reflect the emotional depth of the film as well as its
  central themes, creating a visual bridge between history and the present.
