description_en: >
  `New design and layout` for the “Eu leio” book series, originally launched in
  1993 by Ática Publishing House. Aimed at young readers, this collection
  presents complete translations of over 30 timeless classics of world
  literature — including *The Adventures of Tom Sawyer* by Mark Twain, *Journey
  to the Center of the Earth* by Jules Verne, and *The Jungle Book* by Rudyard
  Kipling.


  The visual identity of the series was reimagined in collaboration with a
  diverse group of illustrators, including **Carlo Giovanni**, **Nate
  Williams**, **Kristian Hammerstad**, **Pietari Posti**, and others. The new
  design brings a contemporary and engaging visual language to these literary
  classics, making them more appealing and accessible to a new generation of
  readers.
