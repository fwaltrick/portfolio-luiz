description_en: >
  `Design of` CD label, inlay card, and booklet for the film "Every Thing Will
  Be Fine."


  Based on a screenplay by Norwegian author Bjørn Olaf Johannessen, Wim Wenders
  tells the story of the author Tomas, whose life is changed by a car accident.
  Although he is not at fault, the incident leads to the breakup with his
  girlfriend and plunges him into a deep depression. Seeking solace, Tomas
  dedicates himself to writing and tries to start anew with a different partner.
  The film explores his quest for meaning and forgiveness, showing that healing
  requires confronting the past and forgiving oneself.
