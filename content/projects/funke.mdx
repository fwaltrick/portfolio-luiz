description_en: >
  Funke Mediengruppe is one of the largest and most influential publishing
  groups in Germany, renowned for its diverse portfolio of daily newspapers.
  Among its flagship titles are the “Berliner Morgenpost,” the “Hamburger
  Abendblatt,” and the “Westdeutsche Allgemeine Zeitung (WAZ),” which are
  respected for their strong regional focus and high journalistic standards.


  Over a period of four years, I had the opportunity to lead a six-person
  graphics team responsible for the production of newspaper pages and special
  supplements for these publications. Our role was multifaceted, requiring both
  creativity and precision as we collaborated closely with the editorial team to
  plan and design new products. This included developing visually compelling
  layouts, ensuring consistency in design, and meeting tight deadlines to
  deliver engaging content to readers. Through this experience, I gained deep
  insights into the dynamic interplay between visual storytelling and editorial
  strategy, further enhancing my expertise in media production and design
  management.

  sechsköpfiges Grafikteam zu leiten, das für die Produktion von Zeitungsseiten

  vielseitig und erforderte sowohl Kreativität als auch Präzision, da wir eng
  mit dem Redaktionsteam zusammenarbeiteten, um neue Produkte zu planen und zu

  Sicherstellung eines konsistenten Designs sowie die Einhaltung enger Fristen,
  um den Lesern fesselnde Inhalte zu liefern. Durch diese Erfahrung gewann ich
  tiefe Einblicke in das dynamische Zusammenspiel zwischen visuellem
  Storytelling und redaktioneller Strategie, was meine Expertise in
  Medienproduktion und Designmanagement weiter vertiefte.
